Our Story

Your Neighbor,
Your Window Cleaner.

Hi there — I'm the owner and sole operator behind ClearView Window Cleaning. I started this business because I believe that great service doesn't have to come with a big corporate price tag.

After years of working for larger companies, I realized something important: homeowners want a service they can trust. Someone who shows up on time, does excellent work, and treats their home like their own. That's exactly what I set out to create.

When you book with ClearView, you're not getting a random crew — you're getting me. I personally clean every window, answer every call, and stand behind every job. Your home deserves that kind of attention, and I'm proud to deliver it.
